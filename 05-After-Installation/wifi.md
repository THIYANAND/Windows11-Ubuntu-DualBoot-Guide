# 🛜 Fixing Wi-Fi Issues (No Adapter Found)

If your Wi-Fi adapter is not detected, you likely need proprietary drivers (common with Realtek and Broadcom chips).

1. Connect your PC to the internet using an Ethernet cable or USB Tethering from your phone.
2. Open the **"Software & Updates"** application.
3. Navigate to the **"Additional Drivers"** tab.
4. Wait for the list to populate, select the proprietary driver for your network adapter, and click **Apply Changes**.
5. Restart your system.
