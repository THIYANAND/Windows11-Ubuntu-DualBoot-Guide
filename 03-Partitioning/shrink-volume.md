# 🗄️ Shrink Windows Volume

Before installing Ubuntu, you need to "carve out" unallocated space from your existing Windows drive.

1. Right-click the **Start button** and select **Disk Management**.
2. Identify your `C:` drive. 
3. Right-click on it and select **Shrink Volume**.
4. Enter the amount of space to shrink (in MB). 
   * *Example:* 50,000 MB for ~50 GB of space.
5. Click **Shrink**. You will now see a block of **Unallocated** space in black. **Do not format this space.** Leave it exactly as it is for the Ubuntu installer to claim.
